# Mixer Node

The Mixer Node can be used to mix up to four audio signals.

## Inputs

*   **In 1**: The first audio input.
*   **In 2**: The second audio input.
*   **In 3**: The third audio input.
*   **In 4**: The fourth audio input.

## Outputs

*   **Audio Out**: The audio output of the node.

## Controls

*   **Gain 1**: The gain of the first audio input.
*   **Gain 2**: The gain of the second audio input.
*   **Gain 3**: The gain of the third audio input.
*   **Gain 4**: The gain of the fourth audio input.
