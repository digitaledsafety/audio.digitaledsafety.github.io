# Tone Generator Node

The Tone Generator Node is a basic oscillator that can be used to generate a variety of waveforms.

## Inputs

*   **FM In**: Frequency modulation input.

## Outputs

*   **Audio**: The audio output of the node.

## Controls

*   **Frequency (Hz)**: The frequency of the oscillator in Hertz.
*   **Waveform**: The waveform of the oscillator. Can be one of `sine`, `square`, `sawtooth`, or `triangle`.
